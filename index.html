We collect and process the following information to provide our services:

We use your data strictly for the following purposes:

3. DATA DELETION REQUESTS (VRC.Privacy.4)

We respect your right to privacy and your control over your data. Any user, regardless of region, may request that their collected or stored user data be deleted at any time.

To request data deletion, please email us at: info.kinetixstudios@gmail.com.

Upon receiving your request, we will verify your identity and delete all associated personal data from our systems within 30 days. No fee is required for this service.
